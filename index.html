Different Types of Famous Flowers

Rose

Location: Asia, Europe, and North America

Season: Summer

Famous for: Symbol of love and romance

Tulip

Location: Europe, North Africa, and Asia

Season: Spring

Famous for: Bright colors and variety of shapes

Sunflower

Location: North and Central America

Season: Summer

Famous for: Large size and bright yellow color
